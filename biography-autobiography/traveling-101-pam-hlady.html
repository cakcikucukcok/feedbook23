Traveling 101 is a collection of humorous and true renditions of many of the trips the Hladys have been on over a twenty-year span. These stories encompass travel across the United States, Mexico, and Germany, and they include camping, skiing, and over-the-road trips, taking place in hotels, timeshares, and bed and breakfasts.

“My, you have had some adventures!”

“Our vacations are dull compared to yours!”
