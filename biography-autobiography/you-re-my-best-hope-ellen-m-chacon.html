When a young woman is faced with family illness and loss, she realizes she must focus on her relationship with God for survival. Though an emotional roller coaster filled with grief and miracles, this journey is inspirational and encouraging for any reader. Ellen Chacon faces losing her best friend (her mother) at a young age. She also struggles with her brothers illness, diagnosed only two years earlier, and fears for his future. With miraculous stories only God could provide, she comes to learn God is ultimately in control of her life. With the love of her husband and three active boys, she presses on and finds peace in letting go, ultimately to tackle head-on another illness that completely changes her life. Youre My Best Hope is a must-read and will inspire any reader to be a better daughter, sister, mother, or father.
