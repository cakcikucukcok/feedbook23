"A Chapter of my Life" is a story of survival and victory despite all odds. This book discusses Kehindes real life experiences as once a victim of abuse. Are you a victim of verbal, financial, emotional, physical or sexual abuse? A Chapter of my Life shows you that you are not alone in the boat.

No one can thrive in an abusive environment. So Abuse is not one of lifes ingredients. It is not what you need..It is a killer disease! Abuse of any kind has the possibility of negatively impacting the lives of individuals, communities and countries; such that it affects childrens lifestyle as much as it does to women. Thus, this book emphasizes the need to get relevant help.

A Chapter of my Life discusses some traits possessed by an abuser; and the need to speak out and break free when these traits are exhibited toward you. It also provides useful guidelines for people finding it difficult to heal after coming out of an abusive environment. The five important steps to take in order to achieve healing, as well as personal experiences through the process of healing have been discussed.
