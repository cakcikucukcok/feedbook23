This book describes the journey of author from India to USA for the pursuit of science and adjustments in social environment. In the beginning, the fellowship offer and acceptance along with the social life of India and USA are discussed. Next, the science is described, and the advantage and disadvantage of moving from one place to another are discussed. Next, the PhD thesis and the family of the author are described. The differences of the kids at different times are presented. Following that, the science and lifestyles are also presented. The authors return to India and coming back to USA are discussed. Following that, the science and lifestyles at Henry Ford Hospital, Loyola University, and Stony Brook University are described elaborately. The major science is explained next. The book ends with tribute to parents and uncles.
