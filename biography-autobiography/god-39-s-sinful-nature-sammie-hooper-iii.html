God's  Sinful Nature reveals how GOD spoke through men, prophets, and  kings; of how His Word will, by all means necessary, go forth; never coming  back void. To Christians, it is a direct door to GOD's truth, both  metaphorically and figuratively speaking. Examining both the Old Testament and  the New Testament, this book confirms GOD's Word and shows you His plan of  redemption. Sammie Hooper shares his testimony of how GOD transformed his life  from the darkest place to His marvelous Light. He exposes some of his struggles  and addictive strongholds that GOD delivered him from. This isn't your ordinary  story. It is Sammie's prayer that this book finds you and ministers to you  where GOD will have it. So enjoy and meditate as this book reveals what the  world calls the Mystery of God's Sinful Nature.

 

Sammie Hooper III was born in Chicago and  raised in the projects on the South Side by a single parent. His Mom did her  best. The poverty stricken area eventually exposed him to a lot of violence and  organized crime at an early age. He chose to become a part of that lifestyle.  He committed all sorts of crimes, was shot at the age of seventeen in January,  and shot again in March. He was then pronounced dead in the Chicago Defender newspaper.


But God had a plan for him, even though Sammie didn't learn that right away. He ended up in the penitentiary three  times, the last time serving nine and a half years which caused him to give his  life to GOD. He got his G.E.D. and earned three months of college before being  released and moving to Las Vegas. He maintains his relationship with GOD while  living in Sin City.

  Sammie had a dream where GOD gave him the  title of God's Sinful Nature as GOD spoke through him as if he was the  vessel with a pen.
