Ignorance is not always bliss, especially for a single woman living and working out of a 10-ton motor home equipped with a panoply of mechanical mysteries and traveling with a persnickety cat. But after an unexpected career interruption Alexis, full of heartwarming optimism, snatches up the chance to live the gypsy life she's dreamed about for more than 25 years. She buckles herself into the driver's seat of her sun-bleached second-hand RV and takes off on a mid-life retirement adventure. Girded with her organic faith, Alexis travels to uncommon locations, meets a kaleidoscope of characters, overcomes an array of obstacles and gains a new appreciation for living in the moment. Her memoir is at times comical, often poignant, and entirely delightful.
