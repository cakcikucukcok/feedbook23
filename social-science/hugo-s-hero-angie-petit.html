On November 13, 2013, young Hugos life changed forever. It was the day he received his service dog, Koda. Koda trained for two years at Autism Service Dogs of America in Oregon to be the most amazing service dog ever.

Koda helps Hugo every single day to be safe, healthy, and independent. From morning to night, Koda is by Hugos side to make sure everything goes okay. Koda even goes to school and rides the bus with Hugo.

Hugos Hero, a picture book for children, introduces autism and the use of autism service dogs. With interactive questions and an engaging childs perspective, it follows Hugo and Koda on an educational adventure of acceptance. It also includes ten tips for incorporating a service dog into the classroom.
