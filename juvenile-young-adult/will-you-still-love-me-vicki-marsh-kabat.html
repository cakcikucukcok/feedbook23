If I use bad words and scream and stomp my feet, will you still love me?

Its the question we all want answered: If I do my worst, will you still love me? This delightful childrens story shows the kind of parenting every child-and the child within every adult deserves.

Vicki Marsh Kabat shares with us the troubling thoughts children can have as they wonder whether their actions and mistakes will cause the people they love most to turn against them. With her child-like illustrations and insightful questions, Kabats story assures children of all ages of an unfaltering love that transcends poor behavior and bad choices.

Accompanied by charming illustrations, Will You Still Love Me? seeks to help every child remember that it is never too late to be loved just the way we are.
