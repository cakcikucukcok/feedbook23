In this delightful series of childrens books, author Harold Krakower brings us into a world of fantasy, play, and wonder manifesting the dreams and desires of youthful imaginings into the spectacular realm of Sugarland. Its Oz meets Narnia in this secret universe of talking animals, princesses, kings, and chocolate lakes. Following Sarah, a bright and daring young girl, on her journeys through Sugarland (and the real world too), the series chronicles our heroines adventures as she learns life lessons and shows children the importance of curiosity, friendship, and generosity. In Josh and His Magic Bed, we meet Sarahs brother Josh who takes his own trip to Sugarland with the help of a magical bed. There, he encounters Sarahs old friends, who quickly become his friends too. Exploring Sugarland and all its characters, Josh grows into an adventurous young man, learning the importance of sharing and family. Children will be drawn to this colorful world where anything is possible and they may be taught a lesson or two themselves along the way! Truly, these stories are a treasure for children and parents alike.
