Albert Pendergasts Peculiar Parcel is a story of how a group of year six students help the class bully to mend his ways. Told in the first person, the story teller has had enough of Alberts thoughtless actions and is determined to get him! Inspired by a dream the narrator tells how he gathers his class mates together to present Albert with a very peculiar parcel. Touched by the contents of the parcel, Albert makes some changes in his life having finally got it!
