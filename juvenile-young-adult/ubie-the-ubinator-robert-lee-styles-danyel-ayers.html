 "UBIE The Ubinator" is the story of an amazing guardian-orange and anti-bully superhero named "UBIE" . UBIE is on a mission to help create more positive energy in the world and help bring an end to bullying, through more acts of kindness, tolerance, and compassion for others. Armed with his amazing secret weapon , UBIE is committed to helping change the hearts and minds of those who are mean or simply being bullies, into heroes. The world certainly needs more heroes. UBIE also protects and encourages children throughout the year, to stay in school and turn away from the awful behavior of bullyinging. You can be sure, that Ubie will bring joy to the entire family and will help create a fun and safe environment for years to come. Remember, that change begins with you, so Be A Hero! "No Buddy Likes A Bully!"
