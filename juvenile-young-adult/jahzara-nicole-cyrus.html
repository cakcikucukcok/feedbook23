Jahzaras First Day is the first in a series of books about a little girl going through life facing many of the challenges that our children face on a daily basis that we dont always realize that they face. Jahzara is 5 years old and her name means blessed princess. In First Day she is going to kindergarten and facing being away from her parents for the first time. She relies on some inspirational words from her father to get her through this new, exciting, and yet frightening experience that all children will one day have to endure. First Day will not only inspire children going through this experience but any experience that may cause them to be frightened, afraid, or a little less confident in themselves.
