Veggie Vero was designed to create dialog between vegan parent and child.  In this way, parents may prepare children for the non-vegan foods they will encounter among peers and provide an understanding of where that food comes from.  In this 1st book of the Veggie Vero series, enjoy 10 adorable color illustrations, a bonus recipe, and behind-the-scenes sketches of the creation of Veggie Vero!
