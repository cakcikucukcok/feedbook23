Includes bonus material:

* A question page for teachers and families to facilitate a discussion about helping out.   Recommended by teachers as being suitable for children from 3years - 99+!  The themes and questions align with the common core. Therefore, this book may be a useful resource for teachers to use in the classroom.

* A page for parents with long term illness entitled "Preparing for a  hospital admission: 5 tips for chronically ill moms."

For more info see https://www.sickmom.org

As a radiographer, Simone Colwill saw patients grappling with what to tell their children and how to reassure them. Then she got sick herself. Super Jonny was inspired by her own journey and the way her son learned to cope.

If there's one book you need to reach for when Mum or Dad is hospitalised for any reason, this is it,
