Poor Cody is having a very bad day. Cody faces one trouble after another from the very moment he wakes up until he climbs into bed. Because hes had such a bad day, Cody goes to bed very sad. Hes sure that with as much trouble as hes faced today his mommy and daddy couldnt possibly love him anymore. He doesnt know how wrong he is until bedtime. When his mommy comes to tuck him in, Cody learns that his mommy and daddy will not only do anything to take care of him, but that they will love him no matter whateven if he has a bad day.

Join Cody as he makes his way through a rough day and discovers just how special a parents love really is. It turns out that a bad day isnt such a bad day after all when you know you have a mommy and daddy to love you.
