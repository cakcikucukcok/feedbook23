During these adventures-as related by young Riley-they encounter legends, meet the makers and shakers of our planet and discover, in demonstration of unparalleled time travel one-upmanship, they can also enter the pages of fiction to hobhob with the likes of Robin Hood and his Merry Band or the likes of Huckleberry Finn!
