What Will I Be Today is a Children's Book for ages 3 to 8 years old. It is about a delightful and energetic 4 year old little girl named Tierry.

Tierry's Granna, a retired educator, bombards her Granddaughter with an abundance of academic learning, art work and dramatic activities. Granna's house is filled with fun and laughter all day.

Many days, Tierry bounds pell-mell through her Granna's door in a costume, no matter what the weather, mimicking her favorite character of the day. She may come barking, singing, growling, roaring, galloping or crawling. She is a happy, carefree and creative child who is dearly loved by everyone she meets.
