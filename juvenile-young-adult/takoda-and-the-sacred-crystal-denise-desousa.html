Takoda, a Native American girl, loves her family, nature, and her way of life. She loves walking in the woods and listening to stories her grandmother has to share with her.

Author Denise DeSousas Takoda and the Scared Crystal, written especially for children between four and eight years old, is about one special mornings walk in nature. On this particular day, the cliff face to the south asks Takoda to come closer. Her family has told her not to go near it, but curiosity is too much for her to resist, and she ventures down the path to the cliff and a surprise.

A powerful book that teaches about the power of connecting to the Earth and her elements. Get your children started early on learning the power of intention and prayer. An absolute treasure!
