This book tells a story about a young girl and her pet pony, their special relationship, their daily life, and their adventure. It also features the girls relationship with her parents and with her best friend, as well as the ponys relationship with other animals, and the ponys own dramatic adventure. The story is imaginative, blending together elements of fantasy, reality, fun and adventure, hardship and problems, drama and humour. It is written mostly to entertain, and stimulate a childs imagination and sense of fun and adventure, while at the same time make them think about practical realities in life. It is hoped that the reader will, in some way, be able to relate to some things in this book. It is aimed to appeal mainly to young girls, and their parents. (Some details in this story have been influenced by the authors own life experiences).
