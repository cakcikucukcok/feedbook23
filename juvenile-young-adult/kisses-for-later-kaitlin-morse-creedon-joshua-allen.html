When Mommy is near, she gives us hugs and snuggles and kisses. But what about when Mommy is gone, and were alone? For those times, she gives us extra kisses we can keep close to our hearts kisses for later!

As a working mother of three little boys, author Kaitlin Morse Creedon discovered firsthand how difficult it can be to separate from her children, even for a short period of time. Giving her sons kisses for later was her solution to relieve these uneasy feelings, and help her little boys build self-confidence and independence.

This sweet story is designed to ease the angst of separation, reassuring little ones (and their parents) that the loving bond they share sticks around, even when theyre not together. It offers parents an actionable way to bond with their children in todays busy world.
