The story is about Buckie and King Levi, two close friends on a journey to seek out the cause of unrest stirring in their part of the region. During their travel, they came upon a quaint small village. The natives of the village seem very happy as they went about their day. Then suddenly, the atmosphere changed when hunters entered the village in vehicles loaded with caged animals. Seeing the animals in cages caused the two friends to worry about the hunters intention. So the buddies watched, listened, and waited to see what the hunters would do. Meanwhile, they crafted a plan of escape for the animals. But to their surprise, what was revealed to the two friends were unexpected and life changing! This story is a continuation of two previous books titled The Mighty King and the Small Creature, and the other, Buckie and King Levi: Friends in Deed. These books were written by the same author, Norma J. McKayhan.
