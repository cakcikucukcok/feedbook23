Count The Helmets is the completion of a 30-year goal to write a highly-accurate and interesting story about the people that were, and are today, the 1985 AFA Falcon football team… the Administrators, the coaches and players and their journey through an extra- ordinary 12-1 season, recalling how their shared experiences at the academy resulted in the development of leaders of character, in a culture of commitment, and a climate of  respect… ready to serve their country.

 

The success of this book, however, will be measured by how well it helps potential  new cadets, from every possible culture and background, better understand the culture and values of the United States Air Force Academy, allowing them to make a better,  more informed choice about whether the AFA is the right place for them…. or not.
