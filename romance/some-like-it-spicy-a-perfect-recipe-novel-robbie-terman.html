Anxious to get back in the kitchen where he belongs, Ty Cates has one more PR commitment --being head judge on a reality show. But the minute he meets fiery Ashton he knows he's in trouble. Head judges dating contestants is about as taboo as putting ketchup on filet mignon, but from the moment they meet, there's a simmering heat between them that neither can ignore.

As the competition heats up, so do the feelings between them and they both fear it could be a recipe for disaster. It's either get out of the kitchen or turn up the heat.
