Come and meet the folks of Fairweather, Minnesota, the small town with the big heart that you'll want to return to again and again!

Dr. Morgan Talbot didn't know what he'd been missing until the day Rachel Anderson White moved back to Fairweather. From the moment they collided in the hall, he was determined to bring joy and laughter back into the lives of Rachel and her young daughter.

Rachel had lost her faith and felt that she could trust no one–especially when it concerned her daughter, Lindsay. Yet Morgan's steadfast care soon began to win them both over. Rachel found herself smiling–and falling in love!

Could it really be true that wedding vows were just what this doctor ordered?
