Andion Kepman is considering suicide, but his plans are briefly derailed when he meets an old childhood friend of his sister’s, Elly. He falls for her and decides not to go through with his suicide plans, but that all changes when Elly dies in an unfortunate automobile accident. She was reading a text…a text sent by Andion.

Andion feels responsible and can’t accept the idea that he played a pivotal role in Elly’s death. Instead, he decides to go through with his previous plan, but not before leaving seven letters, one each to his mother, father, brother, sister, grandmother, and two of his closest friends. Then Andion goes to a river and drugs himself, battling his thoughts until he passes out—and leaving his family to deal with his ruinous choices.
