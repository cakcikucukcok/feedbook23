When Clay & Lynn Sinclair inherit the home of Lynn's great-aunt Mitzi upon her death, they discover a mysterious box of keys hidden away with a cipher meant just for them. After decoding the cipher, they discover that the keys will open something in every state in the U.S. and Washington D.C. Before their deaths, Mitzi and her husband, Sid, created the adventure of a lifetime for Clay and Lynn.

Newly retired, but still young enough for adventure, the Sinclairs embark on the quest that was left for them. Starting in their home state of Louisiana and working their way to each state in the order Aunt Mitzi intended, they head east and up the Atlantic coast.

As they figure out the clues from the cipher along the way and collect items in each place where the keys fit, they learn things about Aunt Mitzi and Uncle Sid's past. They find answers they didn't know they were looking for in their own lives. Lynn discovers what she needs within herself to finally express her innermost secrets to Clay, while still wondering what he's keeping from her.

Book one of this series ends in Maine at a dead end, and the Sinclairs don't know where to turn. How will they continue without this piece of the puzzle? What other secrets lie ahead that will affect their lives?
