A thrilling short story from international bestselling author Jackie Collins, The Power Trip Prequel introduces you to the compelling cast of characters from Jackie's upcoming book The Power Trip (to be published in February 2013).

In Paris you'll meet maverick journalist Flynn Hudson, recently returned from a challenging assignment in Afghanistan. Across town, beautiful supermodel Bianca is playing, while her billionaire Russian lover Aleksandr is planning the ultimate birthday party for her on his luxury super yacht. Five superstar couples will accompany them.

You'll also meet Russian mobster Sergei Zukov, a dangerous man who is on a mission to find out who arranged the mysterious death of his brother. The answer could prove deadly for the glamorous power couples bound for the trip of their lives...
