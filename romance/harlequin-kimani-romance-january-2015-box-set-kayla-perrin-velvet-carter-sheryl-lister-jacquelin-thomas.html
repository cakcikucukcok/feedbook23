FLAMES OF PASSION (Love on Fire)

When Nya Lowe sees her deceitful ex at a party, she does something totally out of character and puts the moves on sexy firefighter Tyler Johansen. As they stoke the flames of a dangerous desire, can they trust each other enough to turn their romance into a forever love?

SEASON FOR LOVE (Kimani Hotties: Forever My Lady)

Velvet Carter

A business trip to Italy with designer Dash Migilio is the kind of getaway that fashion tycoon Lark Randolph really needs. As they explore Italy's beautiful countryside, will the sinfully seductive younger millionaire awaken a passion that tempts Lark to cross the line from business into pleasure?

Sheryl Lister

Declaring a "dating hiatus" is easy decision for teacher Karen Morris as she unwinds solo on a luxurious Caribbean cruise, but businessman Damian Bradshaw is determined to change her mind.
