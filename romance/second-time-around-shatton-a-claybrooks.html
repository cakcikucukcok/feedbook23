Meet former love birds Taylor & Brandon Dr. Taylor Spencer is a very attractive Senior Editor for Motown Magazine, a preeminent lifestyle magazine for African-American men and women, headquartered in downtown Detroit, Michigan. Brandon Jefferson is a gorgeous Head Chef and up and coming entrepreneur, who is opening his own healthier eating lifestyle restaurant, BJs in downtown Detroit, MI. Taylor and Brandon had a one of a kind love storythat is until just days before their wedding, Brandon broke Taylor Spencers heart and walked out of her life, due to fear of family dysfunctions and long term commitment. It took Taylor two years to recover! However, after five years of healing from this tremendous break-up, Taylor has FINALLY picked up the pieces to her heart, and is now engaged to marry another man. Meet Aaron Williams.the man responsible for helping Taylor to trust, again. Aaron Williams is a very handsome Senior Account Manager for General Motors. He is also Taylor Spencers fianc and very loving boyfriend of 3 years. Nevertheless, will the suspicious return of Brandon...just weeks before Taylors wedding to Aaron, halt their plans? Or, has the paths of these two former lovebirds finally washed to shore?
