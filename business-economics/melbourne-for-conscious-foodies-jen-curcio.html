Melbourne for Conscious Foodies by Decisive Cravings is a food guide for locals and travellers … with a difference.

A first of its kind, the guide helps you seek out eateries that support local producers, have a commitment to the environment, sustainablilty, and social good. It also includes tips and places to volunteer in Melbourne’s food community.

Read reviews on coffee, breakfast, lunch, sweets and dinner spots all around Melbourne. The reviews focus on good food and the conscious initiatives of each eatery such as 'Rudimentary' in Footscray who ...'collect all the rainwater from the building. Due to the tanks’ 20,000 litre capacity, they only need main water for an estimated 50 days a year.'

This is a guide that gives back - 25 percent of the profits from each copy sold will be donated back to a Melbourne cause.
