With the growing number of people that are giving up on their dreams, allowing excuses and their circumstances to hold them back, this book brings attention to the many challenges that individuals face on their journey of creating the life they desire. Michelle Watson in this book provides examples, stories, facts and experiences on how to rise above those hurdles and create your desired life. This book will assist you in no longer settling for second best but instead to live in your passion by pushing beyond the excuses and taking the actions necessary to achieve success.

 

 

 

LEARN HOW...

To take hold of your future, overcome excuses, let go of the past, develop the courage to take action and create the life you desire in this mind-blowing journey.

 

FROM THIS BOOK, YOU WILL UNCOVER...

  • Never let past or challenges hold you back or determine your future.
  • How to figure out your passion which determines and defines who you truly are.
  • Life throws many hurdles at you; whether you are going to use them as a reason to fail or as a reason to persevere.
  • Develop a healthy mindset and remain hopeful against all the odds.
  • Never allow yourself to be deceived by fear. Embrace faith and take action.
  • Success does not define you but instead you define success.
  • The pathway to success lies in making the right choices, as your decision today determines the outcome of tomorrow.
  • Perseverance is the key to getting the results you need, so never give up after you have stumbled. Pick yourself up, examine what made you fall and learn from your mistakes.
  • A great mind sees solutions, so in every circumstance focus on the way to overcome problems, rather than obsessing over what caused them.
  • Be strong; recognise your weaknesses and work on them. Figure out your strengths and develop them.
  • Someone else’s story can be a source of strength and encouragement.
  • Why action is better than making excuses.
