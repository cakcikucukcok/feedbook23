Are you worried that life and the passage of time have sent you far past your prime? Discover how to overcome your desire for perfection and celebrate your true beauty.

Are you self-conscious about your imperfections? Are you frustrated with your aging body? Are you afraid of facing death? A terrifying medical diagnosis forced bestselling author Jarem Sawatsky to re-evaluate his life. But after realizing he could no longer live up to society’s impossible standards, Sawatsky learned how to embrace the life he has left. Follow his lead to see the beauty within and around you.

When Sawatsky was diagnosed with a progressive and fatal disease, he experienced the pain of losing his claim to look “normal.” But after coming to terms with his future, he began to challenge society’s assumptions about beauty. By examining and learning from his own experiments to live in a healthier, happier way, you can unlock your own potential too.

In Living the Beauty of Imperfection, you’ll discover:

  • How imperfections, impermanence, and decay can be recognized and celebrated
  • Heartbreaking and humorous stories from the personal journeys of Sawatsky and others
  • Contemporary takes on perfection and love from the teachings of Buddha, Jesus, and Gandhi
  • Practical tools to embrace your beauty and radiance in the midst of major life challenges, and much, much more!

Accompanied by the wit and style Sawatsky displayed in his award-winning Dancing with Elephants, you’ll learn how to better connect with the present. If you like hard-won wisdom, inspirational stories, and mindful living, then you’ll love Jarem Sawatsky’s transformative book.

Buy Living the Beauty of Imperfection to discover your true beauty that lies within and all around!
