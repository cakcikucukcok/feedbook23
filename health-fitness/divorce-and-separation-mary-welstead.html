Relationship breakdown is common and can be extremely harrowing, especially if children are involved. If you're experiencing problems in your relationship, read this book before you make any decision you might later regret. Divorce and Separation explains the legal processes involved in ending a relationship in England and Wales, whether you are married or a civil partner. Topics include the legal options for divorce, dissolution and separation; decrees and orders of nullity; advice for same-sex couples; property and financial matters; your children's best interests; and how to face the future.
