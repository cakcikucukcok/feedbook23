This is a love story set in the turbulent times surrounding the First World War (1914-1918) that is based on a true story involving T.E. Lawrence (Lawrence of Arabia), Zionist rebels in Ottoman controlled Palestine and an unintentional romance between a beautiful Jewish spy and a Turkish General. Through the medium of flashbacks a journalists investigations carries the audience from one clue to another in order to unravel the mystery surrounding the disappearance of a diplomat on a peace mission years after the Great War had ended. The conclusion ties the mystery pieces together and closes the pages on an epic romance that tipped the scales in favor of the Allied war efforts and wrote the Aaronson name into history forever.
