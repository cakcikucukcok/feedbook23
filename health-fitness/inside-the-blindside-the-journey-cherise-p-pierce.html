Cherise P. Pierce, a mother, author, online columnist, writer, mentor and speaker, who focuses on exposing hidden truths and the complexity of personal and professional relationships. Studies are in Journalism, which fueled her passion for writing and the founder of her Single Moms Lets Talk online column www.singlemomsletstalk.com. An aspiration of hers is to have her own clothing line that sends a positive message of inner strength. Make a statement without saying a word, is her mission, while communicating a message to motivate, empower and enlighten young and mature women all over. The contents of this book is what she wished was around before she was pregnant at eighteen, to give her straight talk and reveal costly consequences. At that age, she said she needed it to teach her what others tip-toed around, other than show her how not to put her standards down. Applying her spiritual sense of knowledge is what keeps her grounded and being street savvy is what keeps her one step ahead of the game.
