Little Panda likes to have play-dates with Cousin Panda. One evening, Cousin Panda does something bad and warns Little Panda not to tell anyone. Follow the story and find out how Little Panda became the Brave Little Panda!

Brave Little Panda is a tool to raise awareness of child sexual abuse prevention; empower children to take action; and promote dialogue amongst children, parents, educators, and providers about this sensitive issue. This eBook is available in the following languages: Chinese, Korean, Tagalog, Vietnamese, and Spanish. An animated version of this story book in English and Mandarin is available in mobile App format.
