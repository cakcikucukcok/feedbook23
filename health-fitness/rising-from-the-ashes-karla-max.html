Over the course of a lifetime, all humans accumulate many different experiences. It can be hard for us to reconcile ourselves a failure, especially if we are taught at a young age that failure is something that defines us. Fortunately, where there is a will there is a way, and we can easily begin to identify all of the ways that we are coming up short when it comes to working toward our goals.
