From Welfare To Wealth is an inspirational memoir about my chaotic journey as a child on welfare, to adulthood where I gained spiritual and financial success. This book was written to inspire women and give them the confidence and guidance needed to not just survive, but thrive in their daily lives. It provides resources on buying real estate, investing, and other methods to move their financial and personal lives forward. I also share various personal experiences that happened over the course of my life, which could have made me stop believing in God and myself, but because of my spirituality, I continued to press forward in spite of the conditions and situations. I know that there are many women in the World who can relate to my story, and are probably having similar experiences in their own lives. From Welfare To Wealth was written to empower the lives of women Worldwide, and therefore will not disappoint its reader.
