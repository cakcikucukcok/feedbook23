This is a motivational book which is the KEY to success in achieving ones' dreams and goals through detailed guidelines, methods, suggestions, solutions and real life examples to take the challenge and achieve ones' dreams and goals. Be it to set up a successful online or normal business, or to start a new career, a new job or a new healthy living lifestyle. It's a motivational book with the aim to elicit a reaction and demand immediate action from the reader to take the challenge and take the first step to achieving one's goals. The purpose of the book is to motivate and instill the drive, the ambition and willpower to succeed through the right mind frame, right determination, right resilience and stamina. The book challenges the reader to aim higher, to go for what they want in life be it a new business, a new career, a new job or a new healthy lifestyle persistently and to stay focused and disciplined until they have achieved their goal.
