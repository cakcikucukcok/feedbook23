Do you wonder if you will ever have love?

Love is not just for lucky people, and meeting your ultimate partner is no accident. The real rule to love is simple: love is drawn to love. Finding love isn’t a matter of looking outside of yourself, but rather discovering what it is within you that’s created the barriers that keep it out, so you can send them crashing down.

In her personal story of heartache, defeat, and finally to deep and fulfilling love, Michelle Rose teaches you how to attract and keep the love of your life, for life.

In Love details step-by-step how to break down your unconscious walls, so you learn how to trust again and know with certainty that heartbreak is a thing of the past. By following this plan, you’ll be able to commit to loving and accepting yourself, so your soul mate will find and commit to you.

"Michelle's coaching changed my life. With her guidance I was able to change my career path, find my soul calling, heal my body, and of course, meet the love of my life” -Erin Dooley

Michelle Rose is an international love and relationship coach who has conducted over a thousand hours of private coaching and taught workshops in the USA, Hawaii, UK and Australia. In this much-anticipated book, she shares her wisdom and the system she created to attract and keep her own amazing life partner, together with case study examples of her clients that will help you understand how to attract a deeply fulfilling relationship with the partner of your dreams.
