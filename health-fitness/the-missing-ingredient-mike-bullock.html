Do you feel like something’s missing from your life? Do you crave something more? If you’re like most people, there’s always something else your chasing: a better job, car, house or the latest gadget. But none of those bring the fulfillment we need to live rewarding lives. 

Author Mike Bullock wrestled with this concept for years. Raised in a broken, dysfunctional home, he spent decades yearning for something that wasn’t there-but should be. 

After a string of damaged relationships, Mike found himself at the end of his rope, prepared to tie it into a noose. It was then, at the place between rock and bottom that he discovered the missing ingredient. 

Once the lightbulb turned on, he started adding it everywhere and the results were nothing short of miraculous. Mike stepped out of the shell his life had been and into the new life, one he was meant to live all along. 

And, now, he wants to share this secret by helping you discover and apply the one thing that changes everything.
