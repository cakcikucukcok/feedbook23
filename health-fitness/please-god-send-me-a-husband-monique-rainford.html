In Please God Send Me A Husband, Monique Rainford-Bourne M.D. explores the complexities of relationships and offers inspirational guidance and engaging wisdom for women looking for lifelong partners. She draws on lessons learned from her personal experience, years of counseling patients in her OB-GYN practice, and interviews with over fifty married, single and divorced women to yield insightful anecdotes from their relationships, both successful and failed, and encouragement for women to trust in God's will.

In sixteen short and focused chapters covering such relationship topics as “Do You Really Know What You Are Looking For?”, “How Will I Know?” and “What About Love?”, Rainford-Bourne provides friendly and practical advice and poses thoughtful questions that will help women better understand themselves and project a better self-image as they seek the right husband. In this revised and updated edition, Rainford-Bourne discusses her and her husband's story in more depth offering the reader greater insight into how she persevered and finally found the right relationship.

With grace and heartfelt encouragement backed by a strong faith and unique qualifications, Dr. Rainford-Bourne offers women a trustworthy roadmap to use as they navigate their journey to find Mr. Right making this book an invaluable resource.
