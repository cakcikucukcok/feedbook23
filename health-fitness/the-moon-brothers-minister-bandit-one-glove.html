HOW MUCH OF HUMAN LIFE IS LOST IN WAITING?

This story of inspiration is based on the truth that 2

children in different families sacrificed their lives in

half a century of separation, keeping the world and

most of their family members from becoming aware of

knowledge that would put several families, including

the women and children in danger by a world wide

syndication.

After one became famous, both were falsely accused of

unspeakable things in order to protect certain individuals,

unaware they were among the people needing protection

from this knowledge for half a century.

IRONICALLY, the famous one died before THEIR

PROMISE TO GOD, the deadline to redeem those

ridiculed souls in 50 years, came to pass. But not before

people world wide came to know and love him.

The Minister Bandit One Glove and his foster brother

became THE MOON BROTHERS, because of their

promise of REDEMPTION.
