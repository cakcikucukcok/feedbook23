You want more financially. 6 Figures by 30: Career Navigation Guide is designed to be your career compass that will reduce detours and propel you to 6-figure success. Throughout Cecilyn's career transition from job-seeker to hiring manager, she's learned some tried and true career strategies along the way. In 6 Figures by 30: Career Navigation Guide, she is sharing the secret sauce your career stew needs to get on the fast track to six figures.

The first step is believing you can do it and the next step is cracking this book open and getting started. Don’t waste another day procrastinating.
