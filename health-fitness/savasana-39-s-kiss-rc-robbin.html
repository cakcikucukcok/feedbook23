Sean is a handsome and wealthy New York business executive, struggling with anxiety and seeking answers.

Alex is a beautiful and experienced yoga teacher, driven by the meditative and spiritual aspects of her craft, and compassionate regarding Sean’s mystery ailment.

In time, she brings to him a love of yoga…

He falls for her teachings…

And then they fall for each other.

As Alex continues to guide Sean in his practice and in discovering his true self, they both soon realize that their journey together is more difficult than they could have ever imagined, as circumstances and dark secrets threaten to keep them apart.

Author RC ROBBIN delivers a timeless and transformative tale of two souls yearning to be joined as one, in spite of the many obstacles in their way. Stretching the traditional boundaries of the romance genre, the novel deals with transcendent themes that relate to our very existence and what it truly means to be in love.

At its core, SAVASANA’S KISS is a story of two people on a path of self-discovery, searching for a deeper understanding of life and the universal spirit that resides in all of us.
