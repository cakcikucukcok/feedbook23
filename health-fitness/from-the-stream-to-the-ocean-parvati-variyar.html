From the Stream to the Ocean is a poetic description of the journey of life. Just as a little stream flows undeterred over pebbles and rocks to join a fast moving river and then gushes forward to join the mighty ocean, individuals also strive to move ahead in life towards greater achievements, success and happiness. What makes this journey enigmatic is the difficulty to understand the true purpose of life and the ways to make it more fruitful. Written in a simple and lucid style, the appeal of the poems in this book is straight to the heart. It will inspire readers to introspect and evaluate their own life journey. Even a small step in the right direction will result in great joy.
