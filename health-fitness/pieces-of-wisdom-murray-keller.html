During his work as a parcel postman in Australia, Murray Keller had ample time to think about important subjects. He immersed himself in the matters of the spirit to grow in knowledge and understanding on a daily basis. In Pieces of Wisdom, he presents a collection of those thoughts. Teeming with spiritually rich insight, the works summarize Kellers research learnings, experiences, education, life, and thoughts about the wisdom of the Lord God. A distillation of ideas from the worlds major scriptural texts, it looks at a broad spectrum, everything from religion, to the internal/external man, the trinity, the gift of life, forgiveness, hell, eternity, and more. Keller, a spiritual adventurer pioneering his way along the old and well-trodden path to his final destination, offers his spiritual thoughts out of love and gratitude to the spirit world and all who have gone before him. He shares a journey to find the real meaning and purpose in life.
