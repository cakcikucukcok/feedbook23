Lelani is a little nave and has been waiting for the right woman to show her what it is to be loved and adored. Her time is now.

Jasmines dream is to have a drama free life. Shes sick of the games and the lies. Thats why she may seem to have all that attitude. Male or female, its all about the heart. Just be real with her.

Moe is the definition of a playa. There are too many sexy women and only one of her. Avoiding commitment like the plague is a way of life for her. Can this playa be changed?

This is the story of three women, okay Moe, two women and a stud. Take a moment to see the world through their eyes. Maybe this will change the way you see the game.
