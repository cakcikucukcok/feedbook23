The heavens, the earth, our human bodies, our various relationships: husband-wife, parent-child, sibling, friendship, are all designed and purposed by God, in order for us to fulfill His will in the earth. Relationships are also designed for us to develop our character, to express the various aspects of our personalities, make our various contributions to the world, strengthen us, bring us joy, fun, laughter and lastly, but not least, to depend upon God, to help us relate well with others, in order to achieve well being, harmony and shared goals. This collection of poetry and prose emphasizes God in creation, the Holy Bibles perspective on virginity, the needs of fatherless boys and girls, in an effort to evoke empathy toward such and the Love of God as expressed in the Holy Bible.
