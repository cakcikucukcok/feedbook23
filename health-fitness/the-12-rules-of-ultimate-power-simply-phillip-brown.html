The 12 Rules of Ultimate Power breaks down key elements of success into twelve simple yet effective stages. The tips included and explained in this book vary all the way from how to be yourself and have confidence to how to control your thoughts to filter out the negativity and maintain a healthy mind. This book works its way through the different stages of improving your mindset by encouraging positivity and learning to dismiss negativity. It focuses on the philosophy behind winning and becoming a leader, encouraging you to be happy in your own unique skin. The points are broken down into finer details such as the importance of acting and performing in a bold and confident manner, as your execution will always be hindered by nerves and the fear of making mistakes, although it is equally important not to rush into things. On top of this, basic personality traits are explained in a sense that you will go much further as a likeable and respectable person with a decent reputation. This book deals with how to overcome obstacles and deal with your enemies in a suitable fashion, giving you the advantage to succeed. Each of these highlighted steps aims to improve you as a person and your chances of success. By following these simple guidelines and being in full control of yourself, you will soon be a powerful and admired character.
