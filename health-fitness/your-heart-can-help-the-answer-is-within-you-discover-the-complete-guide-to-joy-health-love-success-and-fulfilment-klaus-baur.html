Your Heart Can Help’ (YHCH) brings together almost 15 years of heart-learning/sharing. Klaus is one of Australia’s specialist in heart/heart-emotional intelligence and heart-capacity building. He works as a consultant, facilitator, coach and therapist.

YHCH is a practical self-help book that explains, in detail, the function of this second or energetic heart in six introductory chapters. The following chapters then offer practical illustration and application in key life areas: family, children, relationships, friendships, health and wellbeing, work, personal and spiritual growth. YHCH is the first of a series of heart-centred books, resources and online training.

The book shares 15 ‘Key Heart Helpers’ and over 80 additional, unique and practical heart-helping exercises to assist individuals, families, couples or friends towards a heart-centred, joyful, love-filled and fulfilled life.  To illustrated the capacity and power of the heart YHCH contains over 40 stories that describe the profound impact of ‘more heart’ in people’s lives. 

It is enriched by 20 ‘figures’, illustrations, images or tables.  ensure that the heart’s capacity, wisdom and day-to-day help is unlocked effectively.  Through all the chapters, YHCH offers a practical, gentle, personal and natural approach to heart-learning, making this book a uniquely comprehensive resource.

Klaus is a community heart workshop facilitator for Padmacahaya, trained by Canada’s Inner Heart Solutions as an organisational heart program facilitator. He completed the US Heart Based Institute course to become a Heart Based Therapeutics practitioner. Klaus has assisted in the establishment of heart-learning centres in various cities in Australia, as well as Berlin and Vienna. He works as a facilitator, heart centred coach, consultant and therapist.
