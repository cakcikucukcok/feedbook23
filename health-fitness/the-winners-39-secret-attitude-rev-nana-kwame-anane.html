Are you in the verge of giving up? Do you want to start all over again? Are you looking for inner peace? Do you feel hopeless and restless? Or do you want to live your fullest potential in this life? The winners secret:Attitude is a book for the young and old, happy or sad, rich or poor, christians and non christians, it is about your attitude.
