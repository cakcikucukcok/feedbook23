3D LIFE is the unique and single book that adresses the need and search of humanity in one book: From fullfilmet in life to prosperity, succes to happiness, values to character, health to habits. They're all present in the " Three dimensional life", wich opens the door to a new way of living. The desire to gain or fullfil our dreams have driven many apart from our most important and precious things in life letting us empty and unhappy. In the race of having we forget about becoming and that's an issue we face along the way  because, altough we fullfil one desire, we neglect our needs. This way of living have driven us in a permanent conflict with ourselves.

Trough "3D LIFE" I'm teaching you a new way of living. A way in wich their dreams, goals and desires work hand in hand along the way with their health and fullfilmet. Everyone of us is formed of three parts: Body, Soul and Spirit which represents the three dimensions I address in my book. When our bodies (which represents our health) is connected to our soul (the source of every accomplishment in life) and our spirit (which determines the purpouse and fullfilmet), than we become "3D PERSONS", meaning Succesul in every areas of our Lives. 

3D LIFE is for every person who desires a complete Succesfull life because is addressing the most important things people are looking for in life. Easy to follow and understand the book have already changed many lives in Norway where I'm currently living.
