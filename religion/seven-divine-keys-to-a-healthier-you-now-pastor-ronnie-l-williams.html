Are you aware of the fact that every

thirty-three seconds someone in

America dies from diseases of the

heart and blood vessels?

Th is book is designed to present God-

inspired truths and the advice of God-

appointed health care professionals to

you in such a loving way that will lead

to a healthier and more effective you

now. The Lord has made it crystal clear

that His desire is that we become filled

with excellent health as we prosper in

fulfilling His plan and purpose for

our lives as revealed in 3 John 1:2

Beloved, I wish above all things that

thou mayest prosper and be in health,

even as thy soul prospereth. To be

filled with excellent health requires us

to make good choices daily in the area

of nurturing our bodies so that healthy

living will become a lifestyle and not

just another New Years resolution.

Again, you shall know the truth

and the truth shall make you free.

I pray that you and your family will

receive these truths, become free from

preventable diseases, and become able

to, as Hebrews 12:1 reveals, run with

patience [endurance] the race that is

set before us.
