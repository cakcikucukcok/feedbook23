Overview

From the Himalayas to the Andes, and South Africa to Antarctica, scientists agree that the remains of a past cataclysm are found everywhere. In this new book, The Noah Code, author Mark Rose sets out to reveal the truth about these finds and much more. A compilation of over 35 years of study and field research, Rose logically and honestly set the record straight about the Flood as recorded in Genesis in easy to understand terms. His conclusions are shocking.

Noah Code speaks a new and exciting message into the Flood account as recorded in Genesis. This volume provides a concise overview of the original Hebrew text, and further seeks to unravel the true character of this man called Noah. For those of faith, Noah Code provides a refreshing look at evidence a century missing, as well as shocking late breaking finds from science. For the curious or skeptic, the volume will likely shake their faith in institutional science and geology, bringing new respect for views once thought religious or scientifically backward. The appendix boasts an excellent compilation of evidence both new and old, including contributions by the founders of science such as Cuvier, Woodworth, Dana, Howorth and even Darwin. Included are scientific finds of modern times, sure to lift many an eyebrow.

Highlights from Science found in The Noah Code

  • In 2014, a mineral called 'ringwoodite' was located beneath the earths crust, containing a volume of water equal to three of today's oceans
  • New evidence from genetics clearly show a population anomaly occurred near the date of Noah's Flood, suggesting that only a few families repopulated earth
  • One fossil deposit in South Africa contains the remains of billions of animals, buried in water deposited strata miles deep extending for 200,000 square miles
  • Early archaeologists identified a clear event horizon showing an extinction layer of man and animals in flood deposits, later occupied by people who were gentile, agrarian, herdsmen and used art. Noah Code documents these finds and much more!
