This book is a manual about the Glory, which will help the readers to get an in-depth understanding of what the Glory is all about. Through this reading, they will understand about God's call to step into the Deep. It is a step-by-step guide that will teach the Body of Christ about how to access the Glory through an easy-to-understand breakdown of the scripture. All readers, from all levels, will have the chance to know more about the call of the Deep. The book begins with a vision about Gods glory being revealed here on earth. Through this, Gods people will be changed. This revelation will propel His people to move forward to greater things, deeper depths, and higher heights regarding God's Kingdom. All readers will receive divine instruction about what God is about to do here on earth and what God requires for us at this time and hour.
