Anything Ive done that was ultimately worthwhile initially scared me to death. Betty Bender

The Hidden Glory Poured Out is a compilation of divine poems inspired by the Holy Spirit through me for other women. It is a breathing guide which illustrates many lifes issues and assists in coping with trials and tribulations that one embark on toward their destiny.

After I got saved, I felt the presence of the Holy Spirit wrapped around me. I was very focused and immovable for the work of God; I mean, I felt nothing would dare deter my focus. Then, life happened

As you read each poem and allow it to speak to your heart, I pray that it reminds you of Gods promises and to be strengthened. I also pray that the Holy Spirit brings you peace and takes you closer to your breakthrough.

I am grateful to God for allowing me to be inspired during my difficult times and in achieving this goal.

Truly those God appoints, HE purges and equips
