Into Oneness, Thoughts and Prayers on the Way is based on the meta-physical spiritual teachings of A Course in Miracles. Its foundation is the realization of our connectedness through the concept of Oneness. Into Oneness promotes healing in relationships and provides a foundation to remember the peace within and how to utilize it to lighten your day.

The paperback book presents 307 pages filled with messages of joy. Every page offers a key to the reader to unlock themselves from this world of illusion and choose peace for himself and all those he meets.

Into Oneness is a very friendly and loving way to embrace yourself and those you love, as you walk together on the path to Inner Peace. Definitely a book to share with those you love.
