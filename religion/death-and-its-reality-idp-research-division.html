Also ignored are the methodology of those who reject the idea of accepting authentic ahadeeth if they are ahaad.

Had they pondered the matters of the Hereafter in any depth, they would have realized that the Hereafter is a different world, and that the standards and measures of the Hereafter are different from those of this world. We have discussed this important dogmatic principle - one of the basic Islamic doctrine, at length.
