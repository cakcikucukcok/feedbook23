From Sinner To Saint is a book that tells about how God rescued Sherry from all of her past and future hurt. She was confused as a child and as an adult heading in the wrong direction. Not knowing what she wanted out of life. She believed that God saved her from living a life of hurt and suicidal thoughts, to tell others to not give up on their dreams. There is more to life than just grief, pain, hurt and shame, there is Jesus Christ. Sherry believed that she now has found love, peace, joy and happiness when she accepted Jesus Christ as the Lord of her life.
