I (Alan Agarrat - the author) am a retired civil engineer and like the carpenter's son - Jesus Christ of approximately 2000 years ago, I take a subtle swipe at "men's establishments". Christ did not have a "Human qualification" in theology, neither have I. My thoughts are likely to be from GOD. The ills of this present world are all due to detrimental thoughts and the question should be: "how or if mankind will correct this?". Some reasons for this lack of correction are implied within. This publication is intended to give "food for thought" and my interpretations of some Bible passages. I trust it gives many people a refreshing way of thinking, ie having fresh "Thoughts".
