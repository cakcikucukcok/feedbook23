Please join us as we walk through the life experiences of just one of the many individuals who desire to express their gratitude to the Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.

Finding grace and mercy; forgiveness and healing, LuAnn Caperton uses her own shortcomings, downfalls, and realizations to show you the many examples of how this God of the universe relates, sees, and cares for even the smallest of us.

At age fifty, LuAnn has chosen to share this Jubilee in celebration and thanks for Gods gift of His Son, Jesus Christ, and the redemption He brings to each of us.

In this stack of letter-filled pages, youll find fifty wondrous stories of how the Lord has ministered to her while walking through everyday life.

Many, O LORD my God, are thy wonderful works which thou hast done,

And thy thoughts which are to us-ward (Psalm 40:5a).

LuAnns hopes are that somewhere in these simple yet heartfelt words to follow, your hope in Christ will either be found for the first time or strengthened in some way by what you read.
