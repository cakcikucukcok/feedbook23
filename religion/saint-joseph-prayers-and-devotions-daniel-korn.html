Beginning with a brief history of the Church's devotion to St. Joseph, this deluxe pamphlet traces the Saint's popularity and importance from early Christian times to our own day. A section of contemporary prayers helps us to understand the special significance that St. Joseph's intercession can have in our daily lives, while traditional prayers remind us of Joseph's example as a trusting servant of the Lord. Feast day meditations and a section of special devotions complete this inspirational collection of prayers to St. Joseph -- a favored saint for men and women of every generation.
