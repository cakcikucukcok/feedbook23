Challenges are part of everyone's life. No matter what one's age, color, creed, or status in life is; storms will appear. The good news is that, as believers in God, we are not alone. He promises to never leave or forsake us. Through the Storm is a thirty-day devotional that helps lift your spirit and remind you that storms don't last always; that the sun will shine again in your life. Dell Staggers has been in ministry for more than 15 years. Dell is a graduate of Luther College. He also received a Masters from Drake University and a Masters from the University of Scranton. He is a former school teacher and youth pastor. The Staggers have 3 beautiful children, David, Daniel, and Faith, and they currently reside just outside of Dallas, TX
