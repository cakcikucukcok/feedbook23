Newly revised and expanded, "Can-Do Kosher!" is a brief yet concise DIY guide on becoming and keeping kosher. The main goal of this handy information-packed pocket-sized book is to make the often complex world of the Jewish dietary laws accessible to anyone who would like to keep them. The author relates the information in simple-to-understand and easy-to-apply formats, explaining the logic behind what is being practiced. Endeavoring to be all-inclusive, she presents information that applies to several different Jewish customs, unlike a majority of Kosher books mainly geared for those with European Jewish backgrounds. Can-Do Kosher is for everyone, and now has even more information than before!
