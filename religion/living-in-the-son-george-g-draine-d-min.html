---- One foggy, misty day in January my wife Virginia, Toni my daughter, and two grand children: Adonis and Brianna left Dawson, GA for Montgomery AL. I would catch a plane to Houston from Montgomery. As we drove down me 85 it was hard to see because of the misty rain and fog. I thought about turning around more than once, but we pressed on, because the goal was to go to Huston to visit her; she was sick. As I was coming aboard Im looking for the pilot. I want see if he was a mature looking person. I want to see a person with a little grey in his head. I want to see if he looked anything like Captain Sully. I wanted to see if he looked like someone that could land a plane on the Hudson River without crashing, What a thought! Too much clutter to communicate with God! Do you think its possible for us to have so much going on in our lives that we miss the things that are really meaningful? Do you think it is possible that we could fail in our communication with God because we have too much clutter in our lives? Jesus talked about some becoming entangled with the cares of the world that they leave the Lord (Mark 4:19).
